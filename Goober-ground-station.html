Introduction

Goober Ground Station tracks and receives data from satellites, later decoding data from them to get useful information and interesting information. The station is funded mostly by me,

Satellites received / tracked / planning Table

The following table lists all of the satellites that have been tracked or receive and or planning on tracking/receiving signals, from GGS.

Satellite Status Received/Tracked Notes
NOAA-15 (NOAA-K) Operational Degraded Yes Its in its end of service period.
NOAA-18 (NOAA-N) Functional Off Yes NOAA permanently shutdown NOAA-18 on June 6th 2025 at 17:33-17:49 UTC, due to a failure in the S band transmitter; reducing to transmitter power output from 7W to 0.8W. Jeopardizing the satellites telemetry and NOAA's ability to control the satellite.
NOAA-19 (NOAA-N Prime) Operational Yes Its in its end of service period.
AMSAT OSCAR-7 (AO-7) Operational Limited Yes Only transmittes in sun light, due a battery failure in mid-1981.
AMSAT OSCAR-10 (AO-10) Contradicting Planning There is contradicting information on the satellites status, and I'm also having a hard time finding historical documentation for this satellite. I'm hoping to at least see it visually.
ISS (ZARYA) Operational Yes The International Space Station, Its planned to deorbit in 2030.
